# Cross-Domain Reframing

## Overview

Applying AI thinking and techniques across different contexts, industries, and disciplines. This pillar is about transferring what works in one domain to solve problems in another — the generalist superpower.

**Community average score: 67%** — Highest of the middle cluster. Users have good instincts here but may lack deliberate practice.

## Three Levels

### 🟢 Basic

Recognizing when a technique from one context could apply to another. Using AI to explore analogies and parallels.

### 🟡 Intermediate

Deliberately translating prompts, frameworks, and workflows across domains. Building a personal library of transferable patterns.

### 🔴 Advanced

Creating novel approaches by combining insights from multiple domains. Teaching cross-domain thinking to teams and organizations.

## Learning Style Fit

- **Natural fit:** [Tinkerers](/archetypes/tinkerer/) and [Social Learners](/archetypes/social-learner/) — experimenting across domains and cross-pollinating through dialogue
- **Growth opportunity:** [Planners](/archetypes/planner/) — borrowing techniques from unfamiliar fields stretches structured thinking
