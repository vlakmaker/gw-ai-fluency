# Ethical Prompting & Judgment

## Overview

Responsible AI use, verification, and critical thinking. This pillar covers knowing when to trust AI output, how to verify it, and how to build systems for accountability. The community scores highest here — but exercises should challenge that confidence with practice, not just awareness.

**Community average score: 75%** — Highest pillar. Users feel confident, but there's often a gap between "I know I should check" and "I actually have a system for checking."

## Three Levels

### 🟢 Basic

Recognizing AI limitations, checking outputs for accuracy, and understanding when AI is not the right tool.

### 🟡 Intermediate

Building verification workflows, designing prompts that reduce bias and hallucination, and creating accountability checkpoints.

### 🔴 Advanced

Establishing organizational AI ethics guidelines, auditing AI workflows for systemic bias, and teaching ethical AI use to others.

## Learning Style Fit

- **Natural fit:** [Planners](/archetypes/planner/) and [Strategists](/archetypes/strategist/) — verification rewards structured thinking and big-picture judgment
- **Growth opportunity:** [Tinkerers](/archetypes/tinkerer/) — slowing down to verify builds a critical habit
