# Insight Synthesis

## Overview

Extracting meaning, patterns, and actionable insights from AI-generated output. This pillar is about going beyond accepting AI answers at face value — learning to synthesize, compare, and build on what AI produces.

**Community average score: 64%** — Most users are past basic and approaching intermediate.

## Three Levels

### 🟢 Basic

Foundations of working with AI output — identifying key points, summarizing, and checking for coherence.

### 🟡 Intermediate

Combining multiple AI outputs, cross-referencing with domain knowledge, and building structured analyses.

### 🔴 Advanced

Designing synthesis workflows, creating frameworks for recurring analysis tasks, and teaching others to extract insight.

## Learning Style Fit

- **Natural fit:** [Strategists](/archetypes/strategist/) and [Planners](/archetypes/planner/) — extracting meaning rewards analytical and systematic thinking
- **Growth opportunity:** [Social Learners](/archetypes/social-learner/) — discussing AI output with others deepens synthesis skills
