# The Innovator

## Description

The Innovator pushes boundaries and experiments relentlessly. You're the first to try a new AI tool, the first to find an unconventional use for an existing one, and the person most likely to break things in productive ways. You're motivated by what's possible rather than what's proven, and you get bored once something becomes routine.

## Strengths with AI

- **Fearless experimentation:** You try prompts and techniques that others wouldn't think of, often discovering capabilities that more cautious users miss
- **Early adoption:** You're comfortable with new and unstable tools, which means you build intuitions about AI capabilities faster than most
- **Agent collaboration:** Your willingness to experiment makes you a natural at multi-agent thinking — you'll try splitting tasks across AI sessions before most people consider it
- **Creative problem framing:** You instinctively reframe problems in ways that unlock AI capabilities others don't use

## Blind Spots

- **Moving too fast to verify:** Your excitement about new possibilities can outpace your verification habits. The gap between "this is exciting" and "this is accurate" is where errors live
- **Novelty over reliability:** You may abandon a working technique for a newer one before fully extracting its value
- **Documentation debt:** You discover useful techniques but rarely document them, so the knowledge stays in your head and doesn't compound
- **Ethics as afterthought:** In the rush to explore what AI *can* do, you may skip the question of what it *should* do in a given context
