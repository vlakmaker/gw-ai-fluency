# The Connector

## Description

The Connector thrives at bridging ideas, people, and domains. You see patterns and relationships where others see silos. In team settings, you're the person who says "this reminds me of something we did in..." and draws unexpected parallels. You instinctively translate concepts from one context to another and build networks of ideas as naturally as networks of people.

## Strengths with AI

- **Cross-pollination:** You naturally use AI to explore analogies and transfer ideas across domains — exactly the skill behind [[pillars/cross-domain-reframing|Cross-Domain Reframing]]
- **Creative prompting:** You're comfortable giving AI unusual or unexpected instructions because you think in terms of "what if we tried..."
- **Synthesis across sources:** You excel at pulling insights from multiple AI outputs and weaving them into a coherent picture
- **Stakeholder translation:** You use AI to rephrase technical ideas for different audiences, making complex concepts accessible

## Blind Spots

- **Breadth over depth:** You may spread across too many AI tools and techniques without mastering any. Multiple tabs open, none producing deep results
- **Uncritical enthusiasm:** When an AI output feels like a connection, you may accept it without verifying whether the parallel actually holds
- **Over-sharing AI output:** Your instinct to share and connect can lead to distributing AI-generated content without sufficient quality checks
- **Avoiding solo depth:** You may rely on social and collaborative AI use while avoiding the harder, solitary work of building automated workflows or structured verification processes
