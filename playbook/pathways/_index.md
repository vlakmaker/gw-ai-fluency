# Pathways

Pathways are guided routes through the playbook. Each one is designed for a specific profile — a combination of strengths and growth areas. Find the one that fits you best.

## Available Pathways

- [[pathways/new-to-ai|Starting from Scratch]] — New to AI? Start here. A gentle, step-by-step introduction.
- [[pathways/strong-communicator-low-technical|Strong Communicator, Building Technical Confidence]] — Great with words and people, ready to build technical AI skills.
- [[pathways/high-synthesis-low-agent|High Synthesis, Low Agent Collaboration]] — Strong at insight extraction, new to agent-based work.
- [[pathways/high-automation-low-ethics|High Automation, Low Ethics]] — Comfortable with automation, needs verification habits.

## Don't See Your Profile?

Start with the pillar where you scored lowest — that's where you'll get the most growth for the least effort. If you haven't taken the quiz, try the [AI Skills Quiz](https://aiskillsquiz.com) to discover your profile.

You can also browse all [[exercises/_index|exercises]] and pick whatever catches your interest. There's no wrong way to navigate this playbook.
