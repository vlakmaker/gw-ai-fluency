# The Five Pillars of AI Fluency

AI fluency isn't one skill — it's a combination of five. We call them **pillars** because each one supports the others. You don't need to master all five at once, but understanding them helps you see where you're strong and where you can grow.

These pillars emerged from research and community data gathered through the [AI Skills Quiz](https://aiskillsquiz.com). Each one represents a distinct way of working with AI.

## The Pillars at a Glance

| Pillar | What It Means | Community Avg |
|--------|--------------|:------------:|
| [[pillars/insight-synthesis\|Insight Synthesis]] | Extracting meaning and patterns from AI output | 64% |
| [[pillars/workflow-automation\|Workflow Automation]] | Building repeatable AI-assisted processes | 65% |
| [[pillars/cross-domain-reframing\|Cross-Domain Reframing]] | Transferring AI techniques across different fields | 67% |
| [[pillars/ethical-prompting\|Ethical Prompting]] | Responsible AI use, verification, and judgment | 75% |
| [[pillars/agent-collaboration\|Agent Collaboration]] | Working with AI agents and multi-agent systems | 51% |

*Community averages based on AI Skills Quiz data.*

## How They Connect

The pillars aren't isolated — they reinforce each other:

- **Ethical Prompting** is foundational. Without it, speed and automation become liabilities. It's also the area where the community scores highest, which is encouraging.
- **Insight Synthesis** and **Cross-Domain Reframing** are natural partners. Extracting meaning from AI output (synthesis) becomes even more powerful when you can apply those insights across different contexts (reframing).
- **Workflow Automation** builds on all the above — once you can think critically, extract insight, and reframe across domains, you're ready to build repeatable processes.
- **Agent Collaboration** is the frontier. It's the newest and most challenging pillar, with the lowest community average. It builds on all four other pillars.

## Where to Start

If you've taken the quiz, start with your **lowest-scoring pillar** — that's where you'll see the most growth for the least effort.

If you haven't taken the quiz, here's a simple guide:

- **New to AI?** Start with [[pillars/ethical-prompting|Ethical Prompting]]. It builds the critical thinking foundation everything else depends on.
- **Already using AI daily?** Try [[pillars/workflow-automation|Workflow Automation]] to make your existing habits more systematic.
- **Feeling adventurous?** Dive into [[pillars/agent-collaboration|Agent Collaboration]] — it's the community's biggest growth area.

Each pillar page links to its exercises, so you can go from understanding to practice in one click.
